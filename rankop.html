    What is an Operator

    One of the reasons why Rainbow has been such a popular game for so many years is because of these Operators. With a multitude of over 40 Operators available, each with a unique specialty, the game is entertaining because of them. Operators are divided into two camps: attackers and defenders. For this reason, in this page the 5 best attackers and 5 best defenders for a lineup will be displayed.
